STRETCHING EXERCISES



Initial focus on how the body work in developing the shot; stance/balance/body position/ stretching exercises/warming up/ alignment and preparation before the actual shooting is very essential.


Archery is a physically demanding sport. Stretching and strengthening exercises are extremely important in order to avoid possible injuries. By using exercises before participating in archery activities, you will improve your range of movement (flexibility).


I don’t want to be considered as a disabled person who can take part in archery. I want to be considered as an Archer who happens to be disabled. - FITA Coaching Manual
