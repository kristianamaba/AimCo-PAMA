PROF. MARIJOY R. SAMSON



She is currently connected at the University of Makati; a local University being funded by the City Government of Makati. She is serving the said institution from 1999 up to present as a regular Faculty member in the College of Human Kinetics. She extends sharing her knowledge as a part-time lecturer at the De La Salle University-Manila teaching basic Physical Education subjects since 2016. She also served as a Part Time Lecturer at De La Salle- College of Saint Benilde from 2005 to 2015. She finished her degree in Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in Physical Education at the Philippines Normal University where she was also awarded as the Athlete of the Year in 1997 for, she was an active Athlete of Archery of Philippine Normal University from 1993 to 1997. She completed Masters in Innovative in Physical Education as her commitment for the advancement of her knowledge and professional skills at the University of Makati School of Continuing Professional Education (SCOPE). Currently, she is now in her Dissertation Writing phase and wanted to give big contribution to the Para-Archery community with her research entitled "An Instructional Material in Coaching Para Archery Mobile Application (AIM-Co PAMA in her Doctorate in Educational Management major in Innovative Education Technology (DEM-IET) from the College of Continuing Advance and Professional Studies (CCAPS) at University of Makati.
